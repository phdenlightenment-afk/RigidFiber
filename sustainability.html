Clear, Responsible Positioning

We'd Rather Be Accurate Than Impressive

Sustainability claims only mean something if they're true. Rigid Fiber is built from paper fiber and manufactured without PFAS or BPA — both verifiable facts about how the product is made. We don't label our products "100% compostable," "zero waste," or "fully biodegradable," because those are specific, regulated claims that require documentation and end-of-life testing we haven't completed. If and when we can substantiate a claim like that, we'll make it. Until then, we'd rather tell you exactly what's true.

  • Paper fiber construction — verified in our materials
  • PFAS-free and BPA-free, confirmed by third-party lab testing
  • Designed to reduce single-use plastic reliance in foodservice settings
